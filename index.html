Destination Tips

Kunming

Green Lake Park (翠湖公园)

  • Best visited in the evening when locals gather for music and dance
  • Seagulls flock to the lake from November to March — a popular sight

Stone Forest (昆明石林风景区)

  • What it is: A UNESCO World Heritage Site covering ~350 km² of ancient karst limestone formations that have been naturally sculpted over 270 million years — often called the "First Wonder of the World"
  • Best time to visit: Arrive before 9 AM to beat the tour groups and enjoy the eerie, misty atmosphere among the stone pillars
  • Yi Torch Festival: If visiting in late July, the local Sani Yi minority hosts the famous Torch Festival here — one of the most vibrant ethnic festivals in Yunnan
  • Ashima Legend: Look out for the rock formation shaped like a young woman — this is "Ashima", a beloved figure in Yi minority folklore who was said to have turned to stone waiting for her lover
  • Two zones: The Major Stone Forest (大石林) has the most dramatic pillars; the Minor Stone Forest (小石林) is quieter and great for photos without crowds
  • Practical: Wear comfortable closed-toe shoes — the terrain is uneven limestone. Allow at least 3 hours. Golf carts are available inside if walking is tiring

Dianchi Lake — Lakeside Walk & Birdwatching

  • Golden Hours: Best lighting and most magical atmosphere for photography between 3:00 PM and 6:30 PM, particularly around the reed beds when the sun sets
  • Bring binoculars — over 138 bird species have been recorded here
  • The western lakeside path near Haigeng Park offers the widest open views

Dounan Flower Market (斗南花市)

  • The night auction starts around 9 PM — a unique spectacle to watch
  • Flowers are sold in bulk but vendors will sell small bunches to tourists
  • Best visited on weekdays when it is less crowded
Dali

Dali Ancient Town

  • The north and south gates are best for photography in the late afternoon light
  • Try Bai tie-dye fabric (扎染) — a traditional local craft sold in the old town
  • Avoid the main tourist strip — explore the quieter eastern lanes for authentic local life

Erhai Lake

  • Cycling the full lake perimeter is ~130 km — rent a bike for a partial loop instead
  • Early morning boat rides offer calm water and misty mountain backdrops
  • The eastern shore near Shuanglang village is less touristy and very photogenic

Xizhou Village

  • Try the famous Xizhou Baba (喜洲粑粑) — a local Bai flatbread stuffed with meat or sweet filling
  • The Yan Family Compound is the most well-preserved Bai courtyard in the village

Yunxiangshan Go-Karting (云想山)

  • Book in advance during weekends and holidays — it fills up quickly
  • Best enjoyed in the late afternoon when temperatures cool down
Lijiang

Lijiang Old Town (Dayan)

  • Wander away from the main Sifang Street — the quieter back lanes are far more charming
  • Morning (before 9 AM) is the best time to explore before tour groups arrive
  • Look out for the traditional Naxi Dongba script — the world's only living pictographic writing system

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山)

  • Altitude is ~4,506 m at the glacier — bring a jacket even in summer, temperatures can drop to 0°C
  • Take altitude sickness seriously — ascend slowly, avoid running, and buy oxygen canisters at the base
  • Book cable car tickets in advance online — they sell out quickly in peak season
  • Best views are in the morning before clouds roll in, usually by midday

Horse Riding at Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山)

  • Rides are guided — guides lead the horse, so no riding experience needed
  • The meadow route offers stunning views of the snow peaks above and the valley below
  • Negotiate price before starting — typical rides are 30–60 minutes

Shangbala Ranch

  • Shangbala Ranch (also known as Shangrila Ranch or Lijiang Shambala Ranch) is a scenic, high-altitude pastoral leisure area located at the foothills of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山) in Lijiang, Yunnan, China
  • It offers vast, open grasslands, pristine lakes, and unobstructed panoramic views of the snow-capped mountain peaks
  • The herbal bath SPA is perfect after a long day at altitude — book in advance as slots fill up quickly

Impression Lijiang (印象丽江)

  • An outdoor live performance directed by Zhang Yimou, performed at the foot of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山)
  • Show duration ~70 minutes; book tickets in advance online
  • Best seats are in the middle rows for the full panoramic backdrop effect

Blue Moon Valley (蓝月谷)

  • The turquoise colour is most vivid on sunny days — plan accordingly
  • Walk the full valley path (about 3 km) to see all five lakes at different shades of blue
  • Combine with the Snow Mountain visit in the same day as they share the same entrance area
Lugu Lake (泸沽湖)

Lugu Lake (泸沽湖) — General

  • Altitude is ~2,690 m — rest well on arrival day and drink plenty of water
  • Best sunrise viewpoint is from the hill above Luoshui Village (15-minute climb)
  • Evenings are cold year-round — bring a warm layer even in summer

Boat Ride on Lugu Lake (泸沽湖)

  • The traditional Mosuo pig-trough boats (猪槽船) are a unique cultural experience
  • Early morning rides (7–9 AM) offer glassy calm water and mist over the lake — best for photos
  • Liwubi Island has a small nature trail — allow 1 hour to walk around

Luoshui Village & Mosuo Culture

  • The Mosuo are a matriarchal society — women own the homes and manage the family
  • Respect local customs — ask permission before photographing people
  • The evening bonfire and dance show starts around 8 PM — arrive early for a good spot
