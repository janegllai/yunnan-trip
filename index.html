Destination Tips

Kunming

Green Lake Park (翠湖公园)

  • Best visited in the evening when locals gather for music and dance
  • Seagulls flock to the lake from November to March — a popular sight

Dounan Flower Market (斗南花市)

  • The night auction starts around 9 PM — a unique spectacle to watch
  • Flowers are sold in bulk but vendors will sell small bunches to tourists
  • Best visited on weekdays when it is less crowded

Dianchi Lake — Lakeside Walk & Birdwatching

  • Golden Hours: Best lighting between 3:00 PM and 6:30 PM around the reed beds
  • Bring binoculars — over 138 bird species have been recorded here
  • The western lakeside path near Haigeng Park offers the widest open views
Dali & Shuanglang (双廊) — East & West

Shuanglang (双廊) — East Side

  • A laid-back lakeside village on the eastern shore of Erhai Lake — less touristy and very photogenic
  • Great base for watching sunrise over the lake in the morning
  • Many boutique guesthouses with Erhai Lake views

Shuanglang Sun Palace — 双廊太阳宫 (杨丽萍艺术空间)

  • Yang Liping's iconic art space on the shores of Erhai Lake — a blend of architecture, art and Yunnan culture
  • Best visited in the morning before crowds arrive
  • The reflection of the building on the lake makes for stunning photos

Hongshi Coffee (红石咖啡) — East Side

  • Hilltop café with a sweeping 270° panoramic view of Cangshan (苍山) and Erhai Lake (洱海)
  • Best enjoyed mid-morning on a clear day for the full mountain and lake backdrop
  • Popular spot — arrive early or expect a short wait for the best seats

Wenbi Village (文笔村) — East Side

  • A charming Bai countryside village with scenic rural landscapes and traditional architecture
  • Quieter and less touristy — great for a relaxed afternoon stroll

Lixiangbang (理想邦) — Santorini of Yunnan

  • White-washed Mediterranean-style complex with terraced views over Erhai Lake
  • Great for photos — the blue-and-white architecture contrasts beautifully with the lake behind
  • The alpine ranch section (理想邦高山牧场) sits higher up for wider mountain meadow views

Yunxiangshan Go-Karting (云想山)

  • Best visited in the late afternoon for the stunning sunset backdrop over Erhai Lake
  • Book in advance during weekends and holidays — it fills up quickly

Xizhou Ancient Town (喜洲古镇) — West Side

  • Well-preserved Bai town famous for traditional courtyard architecture
  • Try the famous Xizhou Baba (喜洲粑粑) — a local Bai flatbread; perfect for lunch
  • The Yan Family Compound is the most well-preserved Bai courtyard in the village

Longkan Wharf (龙龛码头), Cai Village (才村) & Corridor Bridge (廊桥)

  • Scenic lakeside spots along the western Erhai shore — good for a leisurely afternoon stroll
  • The Corridor Bridge offers pretty views over the reed beds and lake

Panxi S-Curve (蟠溪S湾) & S-Curve Viral Stretch (S湾网红段)

  • One of the most photographed roads in Dali — winding lakeside road hugging the Erhai shoreline
  • Best in late afternoon light; the viral stretch is a favourite for drone shots and reels

Haishe Park (海舌公园)

  • A narrow peninsula wetland jutting into Erhai Lake — surrounded by water on three sides
  • Best visited at dusk for golden-hour light on the water before heading to dinner

Dali Ancient Town (大理古镇)

  • The north and south gates are best for photography in the late afternoon light
  • Try Bai tie-dye fabric (扎染) — a traditional local craft sold in the old town
  • Avoid the main tourist strip — explore the quieter eastern lanes for authentic local life
  • Stay in a hotel near the old town to avoid driving after dinner
En Route — Shaxi Ancient Town (沙溪古镇)

Shaxi Ancient Town (沙溪古镇)

  • A remarkably well-preserved ancient market town on the old Tea Horse Road (茶马古道) — one of the most authentic in Yunnan
  • The central Sideng Square (寺登街) is the only surviving ancient market square on the Tea Horse Road
  • Far less touristy than Dali or Lijiang — a peaceful and genuine experience
  • Great spot for lunch — try local Bai dishes and fresh goat cheese (羊乳饼)
  • Allow 1–1.5 hours for a relaxed stroll and lunch before continuing to Lijiang
Lijiang

Lijiang Old Town (Dayan)

  • Wander away from the main Sifang Street — the quieter back lanes are far more charming
  • Morning (before 9 AM) is the best time to explore before tour groups arrive
  • Look out for the traditional Naxi Dongba script — the world's only living pictographic writing system

Naxi Ancient Music (纳西古乐)

  • A living tradition — performed by elderly Naxi musicians preserving music over 500 years old
  • Performed nightly in Lijiang Old Town — book tickets in advance or at the venue
  • Show duration ~90 minutes; best experienced after dinner in the old town

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain (玉龙雪山) Scenic Area

  • The scenic area includes Impression Lijiang, horse riding meadows and Blue Moon Valley
  • Impression Lijiang show runs morning sessions (~10 AM) — book tickets in advance online
  • Best views of the snow peaks are in the morning before clouds roll in

Horse Riding at Jade Dragon Snow Mountain

  • Rides are guided — no riding experience needed
  • The meadow route offers stunning views of the snow peaks above and the valley below
  • Negotiate price before starting — typical rides are 30–60 minutes

Shangbala Ranch (香巴拉牧场)

  • Scenic high-altitude pastoral area at the foothills of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain
  • The herbal bath SPA is perfect after a long day — book in advance as slots fill up quickly

Impression Lijiang (印象丽江)

  • Outdoor live performance directed by Zhang Yimou at the foot of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain
  • Morning sessions available (~70 min) — book tickets in advance online
  • Best seats are in the middle rows for the full panoramic backdrop effect

Blue Moon Valley (蓝月谷)

  • The turquoise colour is most vivid on sunny days — plan accordingly
  • Walk the full valley path (about 3 km) to see all five lakes at different shades of blue
  • Shares the same entrance area as the Snow Mountain scenic zone

Baisha Ancient Town (白沙古镇)

  • A quiet Naxi village at the foot of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain — less touristy than Lijiang Old Town
  • Yundian Coffee is a popular café with snow mountain views — great for a late afternoon stop
  • Good dinner options with a relaxed village atmosphere before heading to the SPA
Lugu Lake (泸沽湖)

Lugu Lake — General

  • Altitude is ~2,690 m — rest well on arrival day and drink plenty of water
  • Best sunrise viewpoint is from the hill above Luoshui Village (15-minute climb)
  • Evenings are cold year-round — bring a warm layer even in summer

Pig-Trough Boat Ride — Luoshui to Lige

  • Depart from Luoshui village pier — row toward Lige Peninsula for the best water views
  • Best water views of the lake and mountains are on this route — especially vivid on clear mornings
  • Winter water hyacinths (水性杨花) bloom on the lake surface in December — a seasonal highlight
  • After landing at Lige, walk up to Lige Viewing Platform (里格观景台) for panoramic views back over the lake

Luoshui Village & Mosuo Culture

  • The Mosuo are a matriarchal society — women own the homes and manage the family
  • Respect local customs — ask permission before photographing people
  • The evening bonfire and dance show starts around 8 PM — arrive early for a good spot
